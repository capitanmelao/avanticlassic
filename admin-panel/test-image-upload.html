  6. Verify the image appears in the preview

🔍 What to Look For

Success indicators:
  • ✅ Image preview appears after upload
  • ✅ No console errors during upload
  • ✅ Image is saved to database with new Supabase URL
  • ✅ Image persists when page is refreshed
Potential issues to check:
  • ❌ Upload fails with permission errors
  • ❌ Image uploads but doesn't display
  • ❌ Console shows CORS errors
  • ❌ Image uploads to wrong bucket
